MY STORY

I’m Ryan Parry, the freelance graphic and web designer behind Ryan Parry Designs, where I create beautiful, purposeful identities and websites for people like you (yes, you! Since the age of 13 I have been creating things. Teaching myself everything from Visual Basic to PHP/MySQL to HTML5/CSS. Creating is not just a job for me, it’s a passion. I have so many thoughts going through my head I can’t create fast enough. If I have a need for something, I want to build it. After graduating from Bournemouth and Poole College and continuing my studies at Southampton Solent University, I realised that i wanted a future in graphic and web design Management. So I decided to start up on my own and now I’m lucky enough to work with some of the most amazing, inspiring people one-on-one, often coming out good friends by the end! Originally from Ringwood, Dorset, I moved to Southampton to experience the startup life first hand.
